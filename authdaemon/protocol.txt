<key> REQUEST <nick> <username> <ip> <inbound (ircd) ip:port> <pass>

- nick is the nickname of the inbound client (if any, otherwise *).
- username -- ditto
- ip is the ip of the inbound client (via getpeername).
- inbound ip/port is the listener that the client connected to
- pass is any password provided by the user

<key> ACCEPT <nick> <username> <ip> <realhost>

- The username will be different if the client had an identd response.

<key> DENY <reason>
